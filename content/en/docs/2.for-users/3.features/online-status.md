# Online Status

A user's **Online Status** shows their current activity on Misskey.
It is displayed as an indicator in the corner of the user's icon on the user page.

Online status changes automatically and cannot be set manually.
You can, however, choose to make your online status to private.

:::warning

Some features, such as user recommendations, may use online status. Making your status private may make it harder for other users to find you.

:::
