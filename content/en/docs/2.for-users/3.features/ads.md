# Advertisements

Misskey's timeline and other features may display advertisements chosen by the instance. You can reduce the frequency of certain ads by clicking the icon on the upper right of those ads.

:::tip

If the total number of ads is too low, ads may reappear even if their frequency has been reduced.

:::

## Setting Up Ads (Administrators Only)

You can add, edit or delete ads in your instance's Control Panel. You can set the image, URL, shape and aspect ratio of your ad.
