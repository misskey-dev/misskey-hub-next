# Clips

Clips are a feature of Misskey that summarize notes.It does not matter whether the notes are yours or someone else's. You can make more than one clip, and you can name them and give them an explanation, as well as manage them.You can also choose whether the clips are open to other users.

To create clips, click "Add" on the [Clip Management page](x-mi-web://my/clips).

To add notes to clips, select Clips on a given note's menu and select the clip to add it to.
